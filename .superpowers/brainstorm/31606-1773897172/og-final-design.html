Design Rationale

At thumbnail: The split layout creates a distinctive two-tone silhouette. Blue wordmark "STAGENT" reads clearly. The right panel's colored card borders (blue = planned, teal = running) create visual rhythm even at tiny sizes.


At full size: The abstract cards reveal themselves as a kanban board — progress bars, status dots, and staggered heights communicate "operational dashboard" without needing to read any text.


Color-coded affordances: Blue = planned/primary, Teal = active/running, Orange/Pink/Purple dots = workflow pattern variety. The 6 colored dots at the bottom are a compact visual signature.
